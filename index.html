Yunhai Han


Welcome! My name is Yunhai Han and I am a Master graduate student at University of California, San diego. My major is robotics and control.

I earned the B.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Yanshan University in China. During the previous four years, I took part in a number of competitions, including China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling(CUMCM), Mathematical Contest in Modeling(MCM), National Undergraduate Electronics Design Contest(NUEDC) and RoboMaster(RM). During these various competitions, I learned a lot about both hardwares and softwares of robotics.

At this moment, I am working for the Autonomous Vehicle Laboratory(AVL) led by Prof.Henrik christensen at the contextual Robotics Institute. My work involves finding an autocalibration method for autonomous vehicle when a traditional chessboard calibration is not available. This project mostly requires the knowledge of computer vision and linear algebra. Feel free to learn more about this project if you are interested in from my Github! Also, you would find the solutions for some courses which I took.
